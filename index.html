Radha Cares

Empowering Lives, One Step at a Time

Radha Cares is dedicated to supporting underprivileged communities through education, health, and empowerment programs.

Our Vision

Founded by Anita Rathod, RadhaCares envisions a world where every child has access to education and support.

RADHA stands for:

  • R – Rebuilding Lives
  • A – Adoption & Care
  • D – Donation & Support
  • H – Hope for All
  • A – Assistance in Education
